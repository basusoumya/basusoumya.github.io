About Me

I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Wireless Networking and Communication Group (WNCG), UT Austin. I am currently advised by Prof. Evdokia Nikolova and Prof. Sanjay Shakkottai. Earlier during my undergraduate studies I have worked under Prof. Goutam Das in IIT Kharagpur.

My research interest lies in Online Learning for optimal resource allocation in stochastic networks. Problems in Wireless Networks, Cloud Computing and Vehicular Networks, motivate and steer my research. I am interested in employing concepts from Machine Learning, Algorithmic Game Theory & Stochastic Optimization Theory in designing scalable algorithms with provable stability and optimality guarantees.
